Recipe #1

  • Time: 30 Minutes
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity

Step 1: Prepare noodles according to package directions. Drain and return to pot.

Step 2: Brown meat in pan until no longer pink. Add taco seasoning and water. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 5-6 minutes, until sauce thickens. Add spaghetti sauce and cook until warmed. Add to noodles and mix thoroughly.

Step 3: To assemble the tacos, place a spoonful of salsa in the shell. Sprinkle with a small amount of cheese. Finally, finish filling the shell with spaghetti. Add lettuce on top, if desired.

Step 4:Very messy, but very yummy!
